Who are we?

IntelligEco is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving accessibility to sustainable living. We do so by posting articles that help educate others on the environment, as well as by selling sustainable products and grouping donation links where you can give to charity.
This organization was started by Ramisa Sharif, a rising senior in high school who is dedicated to the environment. This website was created as a final project for KKCF.
